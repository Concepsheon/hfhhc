Programs

Thank you for your interest in becoming a Habitat homeowner. We believe that everyone should have a safe, affordable place to call home.

Homeownership Program

Prospective Habitat homeowners must demonstrate a need for safe, affordable housing, must partner with Habitat and must be able and willing to pay an affordable mortgage.

Home Repair Program

Own your own home and interested in repairing it? To qualify, the applicant must have a clear title and meet the income requirements including owning their home.
